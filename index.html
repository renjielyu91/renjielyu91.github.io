About me

I am currently a postdoc at Morningside Center of Mathematics. My research lies in algebraic cycles, Hodge theory, moduli space, hyperKaehler variety, abelian variety and cubic hypersurface. In 2020 I received my PhD at University of Amsterdam, under the supervision of Mingmin Shen.
