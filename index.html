I am currently a postdoc at Morningside Center of Mathematics. My research mentor is Baohua Fu. I am interested in studying algebraic varieties by Hodge theory. My research work includes weak Torelli theorem, Chow groups of cubic hypersurface and related hyper-Kaehler variety, degeneration of Hodge structures. I am also interested in motives and motivic Galois groups. I received my PhD at University of Amsterdam, under the supervision of Mingmin Shen.
